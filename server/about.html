About learning how to do this stuff

Deploying with Express feels akin to embarking on a harrowing odyssey through the uncharted wilderness of web development. Each step forward is met with the twisted vines of configuration errors, the steep cliffs of dependency management, and the shadowy beasts of server compatibility issues that lurk, waiting to devour hours of progress. It's a journey fraught with trials, where the promise of a smoothly running app on the distant horizon fuels the weary traveler. Despite the guidance of countless predecessors, marked by their documentation and forums like torches in the dark, the path remains treacherous. The ordeal tests not just technical skill, but the very resolve to push through despair, to emerge, battle-scarred but victorious, with the knowledge that what was once an insurmountable challenge is now a conquered peak in the landscape of a developer's journey.
